# Research result

The known general upper bound gives \(Z(P(n,5))\le12\). Previously, equality was known for
the arithmetic subfamily \(P(24r,5)\).

This package proves equality for every order \(n\ge29\). The lower bound uses exact
half-set separator certificates only at the five consecutive orders \(29,\ldots,33\);
the five-column minor then transfers them to all larger orders.

The half-boundary threshold is sharp at 29, but the zero forcing numbers below 29 are not
claimed here.
