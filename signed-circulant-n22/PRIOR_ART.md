# Prior-art scope

Audit date: 2026-07-23.

Suvagiya's [arXiv:2607.18334v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18334) states the
all-even-\(n\) claim as Conjecture 3 and reports floating-point exhaustive
checks only for \(n\in\{8,10,12,14,16,18\}\).

The neighboring local directory `signed-circulant/` gives an exact \(n=20\)
certificate. This directory is the separate exact \(n=22\) finite gate.

This is not a literature-wide novelty claim and does not prove the conjecture
for all even \(n\).
