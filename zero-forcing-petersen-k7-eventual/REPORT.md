# Research result

The known general upper bound gives \(Z(P(n,7))\le16\). The literature search recorded in
`PRIOR_ART.md` found no previous eventual exact theorem for fixed \(k=7\).

This package proves \(Z(P(n,7))=16\) for \(45\le n\le48\) and for every \(n\ge50\).
The eventual lower bound uses exact half-set separator certificates at
\(n=50,\ldots,56\); the seven-column minor transfers them to all larger orders.

The explicit \(P(49,7)\) half-set has exact internal boundary 14. It is a positive control
and shows why the consecutive base block cannot begin at 49 through this separator
argument. It does not determine \(Z(P(49,7))\).
