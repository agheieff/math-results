# Research result

The three-column reduction used for \(P(n,3)\) is the \(k=3\) instance of a general
\(k\)-column topological-minor construction. The same package gives a transparent symbolic
schedule for the known upper bound \(Z(P(n,k))\le2k+2\).

The useful consequence is finite transfer: \(k\) consecutive pathwidth lower certificates
at any orders \(B,\ldots,B+k-1\), with \(B\ge2k+1\), prove

\[
Z(P(n,k))=2k+2\qquad(n\ge B).
\]

No lower bound for those base orders is asserted here.
