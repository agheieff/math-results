# Math results

Only conclusive results are listed. When a later result implies earlier entries, only the
strongest result is retained.

- Every tournament on at most \(13\) vertices has a strong Seymour vertex
  ([package](strong-seymour/)) — **verified**.
- No clean tight union-closed family on \([8]\), containing \(\varnothing\), has between
  \(2\) and \(23\) members ([packages](union-closed-tight/)) — **verified**.
- For odd \(k\ge9\), the Ghafari--Wanless square \(H_{4k}\) has four transversals with
  empty common intersection and no two disjoint transversals
  ([package](latin-transversals/)) — **verified**.
- The forced-fourth-entry claim for \(G_{38}\) is false
  ([package](latin-transversals/)) — **refuted**.
- Every \(\tau_k\)-maximal simple \(n\)-vertex graph, for \(k\ge1\) and \(n\ge2k+2\),
  has exactly \((k+1)(n-1)-1\) edges ([package](tau-k-maximal/)) — **verified**.
- A size-\(n\) unique-multiset-sum family in a finite abelian group forces group order
  at least \(2^{n-1}\), sharply; in \(\mathbb Z_N\), it forces
  \(N\ge2^{n-1}+2\lfloor(n-1)/2\rfloor\)
  ([packages](unique-multiset-sum/)) — **verified**.
- The degree-9 Hadwiger matching-completion lemma is false, as shown by the
  \(3\times3\) rook graph
  ([package](hadwiger/k7-matching-refutation/)) — **refuted**.
- For \(n\in\{20,22,24,26,28\}\), the minimum signed spectral radius of
  \(C_n(1,2)\) is \(2\sqrt{\cos^2(\pi/n)+\cos^2(2\pi/n)}\), attained by exactly
  the two twisted switching classes
  ([packages](signed-circulant/)) — **verified**.
- \(Z(M(C_q))=5\) for every \(q\ge4\); \(Z(M(P_2))=2\), and \(Z(M(P_n))=3\)
  for every \(n\ge3\) ([packages](zero-forcing-mycielski/)) — **verified**.
- The normalized \(H_3\) root configuration has \(20\) zero-sum triples and
  zero-sum-free independence number \(20\)
  ([package](spherical-zero-sum-h3/)) — **verified**.
- Every \(3\)-uniform family on at most six vertices satisfies the Duval--Reiner
  inequalities for \(r=3,4\) ([package](duval-reiner-r3-r4/)) — **verified**.
- Every \(K_n\) with \(n>7\), after at most five edge deletions, is determined by every
  Laplacian hook-immanantal polynomial
  ([package](laplacian-hook/all-hooks-five-deletions/)) — **verified**.
- Li--Wang's displayed three-cycle hook-character binomial difference is incorrect
  ([package](laplacian-hook/all-hooks-five-deletions/)) — **refuted**.
- The six-pair branch-set reduction for the 13-vertex Hadwiger base case is false
  ([package](hadwiger/alpha2-chi7/)) — **refuted**.
- Universal all-hook determination after six edge deletions is false
  ([package](laplacian-hook/all-hooks-six-refutation/)) — **refuted**.
- \(Z(P(n,k))=2k+2\) in the certified ranges: \(k=3,n\ge13\);
  \(k=4,n\ge18\); \(k=5,n\ge29\); \(k=6,n=35\) or \(n\ge37\);
  \(k=7,45\le n\le48\) or \(n\ge50\); and \(k=8,n\ge65\)
  ([packages](zero-forcing-petersen/)) — **verified**.
- The finitary zero forcing number of the bi-infinite generalized Petersen strip with
  inner step three is \(8\)
  ([package](zero-forcing-petersen/k3-infinite/)) — **verified**.
- Every half-set of \(P(n,3)\), for \(n\ge17\), has internal boundary at least \(8\)
  ([package](zero-forcing-petersen/k3-separator/)) — **verified**.
- \(Z(P(n,k))\le2k+2\) for \(k\ge1\) and \(n\ge2k+1\)
  ([package](zero-forcing-petersen/general-transfer/)) — **verified**.
- The \(k\)-column minor transfer propagates \(k\) consecutive lower certificates to
  all larger orders
  ([package](zero-forcing-petersen/general-transfer/)) — **verified**.
- Every half-set of \(P(n,4)\), for \(n\ge23\), has internal boundary at least \(10\),
  sharply ([package](zero-forcing-petersen/k4-separator/)) — **verified**.
- Deleting a nonedge from a \((2k-1)\)-vertex \(k\)-critical graph with independence
  number two need not leave a \((k-1)\)-critical graph
  ([package](hadwiger/alpha2-order31/)) — **refuted**.
- The forced \(K_9-e\) in a hypothetical 33-vertex independence-two Hadwiger
  counterexample has an outside edge dominating its seven-clique
  ([package](hadwiger/alpha2-order33/)) — **verified**.
- For odd \(n\ge9\), deleting at most eleven edges from \(K_n\) leaves a graph
  determined by its self-conjugate Laplacian hook-immanantal polynomial
  ([package](laplacian-hook/exceptional-eleven-deletions/)) — **verified**.
- A 35-vertex, clique-nine, 18-contraction-critical graph need not have an outside edge
  dominating a fixed \(K_9\)
  ([package](hadwiger/alpha2-order35-omega9/)) — **refuted**.
- Every graph on at most \(38\) vertices with independence number at most two satisfies
  Hadwiger's conjecture ([package](hadwiger/alpha2-order37/)) — **verified**.
- The listed local order-39, clique-ten support conditions do not force an outside edge
  dominating the fixed \(K_{10}\)
  ([package](hadwiger/alpha2-order39-support-refutation/)) — **refuted**.
- For \(k,m\ge3\), \(P(km,k)\) has a half-set with internal boundary exactly \(2m\)
  ([package](zero-forcing-petersen/all-k-boundary/)) — **verified**.
- Hiary's Turing-checked table contains consecutive zeros with exactly five intervening
  ordinary Gram points whose real-zeta signs are \(+,-,+,-,+\)
  ([package](riemann-five-gram-run/)) — **verified**.
