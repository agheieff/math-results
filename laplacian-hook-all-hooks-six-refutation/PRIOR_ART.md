# Prior-art scope

Audit date: 2026-07-23.

[Li and Wang, arXiv:2607.19411v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19411v1)
prove their determination theorem for complements with at most five edges
away from the exceptional line. This lane tests the superficially natural
replacement of five by six and gives an exact \(k=1\) refutation.

No broader literature novelty claim is made.
