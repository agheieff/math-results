# Prior-art audit

Audit date: 2026-07-23.

Annor and Howerton,
[*Induced Subgraph Bounds on the Zero Forcing Number and a
\((\chi,\omega,Z)\)-Conjecture*](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20137v1),
propose

\[
\chi(G)\le
\left\lceil\frac{\omega(G)+Z(G)+1}{2}\right\rceil.
\]

Their finite Mycielskian experiments concern \(M(C_5),\ldots,M(C_{13})\)
and \(M^2(K_3)\), not the path family proved here.

The nullity bound

\[
\operatorname{null}(A)\le Z(G)\qquad(A\in\mathcal S(G))
\]

comes from the AIM Minimum Rank--Special Graphs Work Group,
[*Zero forcing sets and the minimum rank of
graphs*](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.laa.2007.10.009),
Linear Algebra and its Applications 428 (2008), 1628--1648.

Exact-phrase, web, and arXiv searches for zero forcing numbers of
Mycielskians of paths did not locate an earlier all-\(n\) formula. This is a
limited current-source audit, not a literature-wide novelty claim.
