# Research result

The known general upper bound gives \(Z(P(n,6))\le14\). The literature search recorded in
`PRIOR_ART.md` found no previous eventual exact theorem for fixed \(k=6\).

This package proves \(Z(P(35,6))=14\) and equality for every \(n\ge37\). The eventual
lower bound uses exact half-set separator certificates only at \(n=37,\ldots,42\); the
six-column minor transfers them to all larger orders. The empty closure at 35 supplies the
isolated equality directly.

The exact search found no boundary-13 half-set at \(n=35\), but it found one at \(n=36\).
The explicit \(n=36\) witness is a positive control and shows that the six-base block
cannot start at 36 by this argument. It does not determine \(Z(P(36,6))\).
