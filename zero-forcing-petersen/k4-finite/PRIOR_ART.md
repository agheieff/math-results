# Prior-art boundary

Alameda, Curl, Grez, Hogben, Kingston, Schulte, Young, and Young, “Families of graphs with
maximum nullity equal to zero forcing number” (2018), prove the general upper bound

\[
Z(P(n,k))\le2k+2,
\]

which gives ten for \(k=4\). Rashidi, Shajareh Poursalavati, and Tavakkoli, “Computing the
zero forcing number for generalized Petersen graphs” (2020), give claimed exact evaluations
for other parameter families; this audit found no exact eventual formula for \(P(n,4)\).

Primary sources:

- <https://doi.org/10.1515/spma-2018-0006>
- <https://doi.org/10.13069/jacodesmath.729465>

The upper schedule in this package is replayed independently rather than imported as a
computational assumption.
