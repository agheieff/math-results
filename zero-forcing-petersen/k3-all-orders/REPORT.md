# Research result

The corrected 2026 finite computations led to the conjecture

\[
Z(P(n,3))=8\qquad(n\ge13).
\]

This package proves the conjecture for all orders. The transfer mechanism is pathwidth:
three exact width-seven prefix searches establish pathwidth at least eight at orders
\(14,15,16\), and a three-column topological-minor reduction propagates those three residue
classes indefinitely. Order \(13\) is closed by a complete seven-set enumeration, while eight
consecutive outer vertices give the uniform upper bound.

No stabilization of the earlier closure histograms is assumed.
