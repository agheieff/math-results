# Exact bounded shell checkpoint

The universal assertion in [ASSERTION.md](ASSERTION.md) remains **inconclusive**.
This checkpoint proves a finite statement about the two nearest nonzero
multiplicity-\(L^1\) shells around `SEED_SUPPORTS`.

## Why the shell generators are complete

Write \(m_0\) for the seed multiplicity vector and \(m\) for an endpoint.
Both have total multiplicity \(28\). If
\(\lVert m-m_0\rVert_1=2r\), the canonical negative and positive parts each
contain exactly \(r\) support occurrences.

Every coordinate of the seed occurs exactly nine times. The frozen
coordinate cap is also nine, so the coordinate-use vector of the \(r\)
added occurrences is componentwise at most the use vector of the \(r\)
removed occurrences.

For \(r=2\), if the removed supports are \(R_1,R_2\), the added supports
\(A_1,A_2\) therefore satisfy

\[
A_1\cup A_2\subseteq R_1\cup R_2,\qquad
A_1\cap A_2\subseteq R_1\cap R_2.
\]

For \(r=3\), the generator records the coordinates used at least once,
twice, and three times by the removed triple:

\[
C_1=R_1\cup R_2\cup R_3,\quad
C_2=(R_1\cap R_2)\cup(R_1\cap R_3)\cup(R_2\cap R_3),\quad
C_3=R_1\cap R_2\cap R_3.
\]

It enumerates every sorted added triple whose corresponding use-level sets
are contained in \(C_1,C_2,C_3\). A support type appearing on both sides is
a cancellation and hence belongs to a smaller \(L^1\) shell; those
transitions are discarded. Sorting added supports and deduplicating final
multisets normalize repeated types and the duplicated seed support.
Every remaining endpoint is then checked against the complete local
conditions and the exact Hall separator.

## Search provenance and boundary

The first fresh endpoint was generated by the exact CP-SAT local model on
the \(L^1=4\) shell and accepted by the exact lazy Hall separator. The
complete replacement enumerators then independently exhausted distances
four and six and reproduced it.

No statement is made about distance eight or beyond, nor about all support
systems satisfying [ASSERTION.md](ASSERTION.md). In particular, this is not
a proof of the universal spanning assertion and not a new result on
Hadwiger's conjecture. Recent background for the order-39 reduction is
[arXiv:2510.12564](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.12564); the shell computation
does not depend on that paper.
